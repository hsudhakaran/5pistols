A LITTLE ABOUT US

How the idea of creating Portswood rum came about was when a small group of us went out to have a couple of drinks. We’re all connected to the alcoholic beverage or exports industry in some way or another, from operations, new product development, international trade, sales and marketing, supply chain and exports, and we we’re sharing some knowledge, having a couple of laughs. When the conversation turned too: If you could start a business, what would it be? We threw around some ideas when someone said: Look, we’ve got all of this expertise, work experience and global beverage knowledge between us. Isn’t there something interesting there?

Someone said: You know, the industry has been speaking for 3 years about the craft rum category being set to explode in the same way that gin did. And you can see it happening already with rum bars opening up in CT and the first rum festival happening this year.

OUR PRODUCT

To achieve real change, we have to expand boundaries. Because the Wild West of what-could-be is unexplored but rife with opportunity.



ONE BRAND, ONE VOICE.


For Portswood we hand selected from credible sources an aromatic bouquet of spices that creates a more well balanced, complex profile, with depth of flavour. But we felt it still needed something extra. Some richness. Something unique. The answer was right in front of us: The Cape Fig that literally grows on Portswood Road.

Cape fig is a succulent plant indigenous to the Cape of Good Hope, and in general its ripe fruits are gathered and either eaten fresh or made into a jam. And we had an incredible aha moment! The cape fig infusion was the perfect fit - providing the earthiness and aroma we wanted, and that most spiced rums lack. And that’s why we present you.
